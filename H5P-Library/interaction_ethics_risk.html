All of these scenarios are a little messy. Focus on which risk is most central given how the customization is being used.

Scenario 1: Drafting Feedback from Full Essays

A faculty member uploads a full set of identifiable student essays into a custom GPT to generate individualized feedback suggestions for each student.

Scenario 2: Policy Explainer GPT for Students

An advising office deploys a policy explainer GPT on its website. Students begin treating its answers as the definitive word on deadlines and exceptions.

Scenario 3: “Neutral” Rubric-Tuning GPT

A department builds a custom GPT that suggests rubric tweaks to “standardize grading” across sections, using only past assignments from one instructor as its training examples.
